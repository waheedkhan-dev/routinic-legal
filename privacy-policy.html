Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 14, 2026

Welcome to Routinic. Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our habit tracking application.

Information We Collect

Routinic is designed with privacy in mind. We collect minimal information:

  • Habit Data: Your habits, completion records, and tracking data are stored locally on your device.
  • Mood Data: Any mood entries you create are stored locally on your device.
  • App Preferences: Your settings and preferences are stored locally on your device.
Local Storage: All your personal habit and mood data is stored locally on your device. We do not have access to this data, and it is not transmitted to our servers.

How We Use Your Information

Since your data is stored locally, we use it solely to:

  • Display your habits and tracking progress
  • Send local notifications and reminders you have configured
  • Generate statistics and insights about your habits

Third-Party Services

Our app may use the following third-party services:

  • Google AdMob: We may display advertisements. AdMob may collect device information and advertising identifiers to show relevant ads. See Google's Privacy Policy for more details.
  • Google Play Services: Required for certain app functionality on Android devices.

Data Security

Your data is stored locally on your device and is protected by your device's security measures. We recommend:

  • Keeping your device software up to date
  • Using a secure lock screen
  • Backing up your device regularly

Children's Privacy

Routinic is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.
